Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA-E)

ARPA-E funds research on and the development of high-potential, high-impact energy technologies that are too early for private-sector investment. The agency focuses on technologies that can be meaningfully advanced with a modest investment over a defined period of time in order to catalyze the translation from scientific discovery to early-stage technology.

Participants must submit a participation registration (Nucleus --> A-Z --> Proposals --> Participation Registration) no later than the dates specified below. Notification to proceed and additional participation instructions/templates will be provided upon registration. The participation registration is required for any INL-led or INL-supporting (subrecipient) proposals.

For more information regarding these funding opportunities, please contact the technical lead, Boryann Liaw or proposal coordinator, Wendy McManamon.
