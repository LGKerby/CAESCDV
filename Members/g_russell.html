Glenn Russell

Glenn Russell is a Geospatial Technical Lead for the Idaho National Laboratory (INL). He has over 17 years of experience in the field of GIS. In the last six years, he has focused on developing expertise in GIS web mapping development and deployment, creative data driven mapping solutions, enterprise and database administration for Emergency Management Operations, Cultural Resources, Geological Mapping needs at the INL. Glenn’s previous experience includes Sr. GIS Analyst for environmental consulting and Sr. Policy Analyst (GIS) for the New Zealand Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry (MAF). Glenn’s is currently developing Paleo-digital elevation model reconstruction of the Eastern Idaho Snake Plain and correlating paleo-topography with genetic variation and geographic distribution of native salmonid species of western US for his thesis in Geosciences.
